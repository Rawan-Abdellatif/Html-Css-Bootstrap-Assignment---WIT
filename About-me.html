Rawan Abdellatif

I am Rawan Abdellatif, a Web Developer with a background in Mechatronics Engineering and a strong interest in building modern, responsive websites. I completed a Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp at Concordia University where I sharpened my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and WordPress.

Before moving into web development, I worked as an Electrical Engineer at Aramco and SABIC. This experience gave me a solid technical foundation and an analytical mindset that I now apply to building clean, structured, and user-focused web solutions.

I am also a proud mother of three boys including a pair of energetic twins. Balancing motherhood and personal development has taught me the value of time management, patience, and continuous learning.

Since relocating to Canada, I've focused on both integrating my family into the local community and growing my professional skills. I am particularly drawn to frontend development and enjoy working with tools like Git, Elementor Pro, and responsive frameworks like Bootstrap.

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring design ideas, learning new technologies, and supporting my boys as they grow and thrive.
